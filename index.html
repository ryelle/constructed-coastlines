Want a higher-def map? Bump up the precision and reload: localStorage.setItem( 'precision', 16 );

Lower it for a faster generation time (but a jankier map). I would reccommend between 12 and 16, higher than 16 will take longer to render. Default is 14, and it's capped at 20.
